Admissions 2026-27

Join the BSVS Family

Simple, transparent admission process for the academic year 2026-27. Limited seats available — apply early.

Admission Process

1

Submit Application

Fill the online application form with all required details and submit with the registration fee.

2

Document Verification

Submit original documents: Birth certificate, previous school TC, report cards, and Aadhar card.

3

Interaction & Assessment

A friendly interaction session for child and parents with the academic counselor (for Class I and above).

4

Fee Payment

Receive admission offer letter. Pay the first installment of school fees to confirm your seat.

5

Welcome to BSVS!

Collect school kit, diary, and uniform. Attend the orientation program for new students and parents.

📄 Required Documents

✓ Birth Certificate
✓ Aadhar Card (Child)
✓ Previous TC/Records
✓ Passport Photos (4)
✓ Parent ID Proof
✓ Address Proof
